OUTCOME
The analysis gave the client several key insights:
1-	Most Valuable Age Group: - The 28-30 age group was identified as the highest spender, so the client could focus more product offerings and marketing strategies on this group.
2-	Underperforming Groups: I identified that the 22-24 and 49-51 age groups had the lowest spending, providing the client an opportunity to make product adjustments or engage these groups more effectively.
5-	Top 5 Customers Identified: The client now had a clear list of the Top 5 customers based on their lifetime value, which could help in future personalized marketing, special offers, or loyalty programs.
